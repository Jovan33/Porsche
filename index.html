About Us

Porsche Penske Motorsport is a premier global racing partnership between Porsche and Team Penske, formed in 2023 to compete with the Porsche 963 hybrid LMDh prototype and Porsche 911 GT3 R (992) LMGT3 in the FIA World Endurance Championship (WEC) and IMSA WeatherTech SportsCar Championship. The team achieved immediate, dominant success, winning back-to-back IMSA GTP titles in 2024 and 2025, including the 24 Hours of Daytona.

Porsche 963

The Porsche 963 is a hybrid Le Mans Daytona h (LMDh) prototype competing in top-tier endurance racing (FIA WEC and IMSA). It features a twin-turbo V8 engine, producing around 680 horsepower. If u want to learn more about the Porsche 963, u can contact us or watch this video. The Porsche 963 typically laps the 13.6-kilometer Circuit de la Sarthe in the low 3:26-3:29 range during official sessions. During the 2024 FIA WEC test day, the top Porsche 963 set a time of 3:26.907. In the 2025 race, the #6 factory car finished 2nd, completing 387 laps and consistently battling for the lead.
