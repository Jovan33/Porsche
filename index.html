About Us

Porsche Penske Motorsport is a premier global racing partnership between Porsche and Team Penske, formed in 2023 to compete with the Porsche 963 hybrid LMDh prototype in the FIA World Endurance Championship (WEC) and IMSA WeatherTech SportsCar Championship. The team achieved immediate, dominant success, winning back-to-back IMSA GTP titles in 2024 and 2025, including the 24 Hours of Daytona.
